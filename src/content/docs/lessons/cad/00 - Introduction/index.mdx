# 00 - Introduction

Welcome to the CAD subteam!

In this preliminary lesson, we will be going over the overarching goals of the program, along with introducing the general structure of the website.

## What you'll learn

The things you will be learning can be broadly categorized into two dimensions:

1. **Macro Design**
   1. **Goal:** To learn the different mechanisms used to design a robot
   2. **Method:** This will be achieved by reading weekly assigned chapters of the Macro Design Handbook (shared to you through your inbox)


2. **CAD Fundamentals**
   1. **Goal:** To learn how to use Fusion to effectively design mechanisms&#x20;
   2. **Method:** This will be achieved by watching demonstrations and following along on your own devices.

## Agenda

This will be the main goal of the lesson will be stated, along with any resources that will be used for that day's lesson.
