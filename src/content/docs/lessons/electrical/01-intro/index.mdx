Welcome to the electrical subteam! We wire the robot: power distribution,
motor controllers, sensors, and the control system that ties it together.

## What you'll learn

- Electrical safety and wiring standards
- The FRC control system (roboRIO, PDH, radio)
- Crimping, soldering, and cable management
- Troubleshooting with a multimeter
